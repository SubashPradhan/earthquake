Nepal Earthquake Help Foundation is the first and largest global crowdfunding community that connects nonprofits, donors, and companies in nearly every country around the world. We make it possible for nonprofits from Asia to Europe (and hundreds of places in between) to access the tools, training, and support they need to be more effective and make our world a better place.

WHAT WE DO ?

Health Matters:
We have been working to improve the health and well-being of communities in the Everest region for over 50 years.

A better education:
We focus on improving the quality of education, as well as training teachers and providing resources for schools in Solukhumbu.

Create Opportunities:
The Trust issues 67 local schools with student learning resources, and provides scholarships to enable children to complete their education.
